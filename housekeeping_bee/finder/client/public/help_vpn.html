Important Notice!

Attention! Enabling your VPN can affect your ability to find the Housekeeper Bee server on your local network.

When the VPN is active, you will not be able to find the Housekeeper Bee server. This could lead to significant difficulties in connecting and using the application as intended.

For the best experience, please disable your VPN to ensure you can successfully connect to the Housekeeper Bee server on your local network.

Once you're done, feel free to re-enable your VPN for security!
